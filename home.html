Local, reliable excavation services. We offer trenching, brush clearing, foundation digging, and more. Serving your residential and small commercial backhoe needs with quality and care.

Our Story

We’re a small family with a big piece of land—ten acres we’ve poured our heart and muscle into over the years. From digging trenches for drainage, leveling ground for sheds, and creating garden beds to clearing brush and preparing foundations, we’ve done it all ourselves with the help of our trusty backhoe.

What started as weekend projects turned into a passion. We’ve learned what it takes to do things right—and now, we’re ready to help others do the same.

A Guy with a Backhoe is more than just a name. It’s a neighbor you can count on. We’re proud to offer excavation services to our local community with the same care and reliability we’ve brought to our own property.

Let’s build something great—together.
